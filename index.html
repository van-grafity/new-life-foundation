Our Mission & Vision

Bringing God's love and transformation to Batam since 2015

New Life Foundation is a Christian ministry dedicated to helping individuals find their God-given purpose through spiritual guidance, biblical counseling, and compassionate community support.

We believe that through Jesus Christ, every person can experience true transformation and live a life filled with hope, regardless of their past or current circumstances.

  • Christ-centered approach
  • Compassionate care
  • Practical support
  • Scripture-based guidance
  • Prayer ministry
  • Open to all

John's Transformation

John Doe

Former Addict, Now Ministry Leader

"I was addicted to drugs for 15 years, lost everything, and was living on the streets. One day, a volunteer from New Life Foundation invited me to their recovery program. At first, I resisted, but their persistent love broke through my hardened heart.

Through their Christ-centered program, I discovered that my true need wasn't just to stop using drugs, but to fill the God-shaped void in my life. The Bible studies and one-on-one counseling helped me understand God's love for the first time.

"Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!" 2 Corinthians 5:17

Today, I've been clean for 3 years and serve as a leader in the very program that saved my life. I'm married, reunited with my family, and most importantly, I have a living relationship with Jesus Christ."

Sarah's Journey to Healing

Sarah Lim

From Depression to Joy

"After my husband left me for another woman, I fell into a deep depression. I couldn't eat, sleep, or function normally. A friend brought me to New Life Foundation's weekly support group for broken marriages.

The biblical counseling helped me process my pain in light of God's truth. I learned that my worth isn't defined by my marital status but by being a child of God. The women's Bible study became my lifeline as I rediscovered God's love and purpose for my life.

"The LORD is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit." Psalm 34:18

Today, I lead a support group for other women going through divorce. While the pain was real, God used it to draw me closer to Him and give me a ministry of comfort to others."

David's Spiritual Awakening

David Wong

Businessman Redeemed

"On the outside, I had it all - a successful business, beautiful home, and financial security. But inside, I felt empty and directionless. I started asking, 'Is this all there is to life?'

A business associate invited me to New Life's men's discipleship group. Through studying the Bible with other Christian businessmen, I realized I'd been chasing success at the expense of my soul. I gave my life to Christ and began aligning my business practices with biblical principles.

"What good is it for someone to gain the whole world, yet forfeit their soul?" Mark 8:36

Now I run my business as a ministry, providing jobs for graduates of New Life's recovery program. My family has come to faith, and we're growing together in Christ. For the first time, my life has eternal purpose."

Partner With Us

Your support transforms lives through Christ

Your generous donation enables us to continue our vital ministry of bringing hope to the hopeless and helping people discover new life in Jesus Christ. Every contribution makes an eternal difference.

  • $50 - Provides a week of meals for someone in our recovery program
  • $100 - Covers counseling sessions for someone in crisis
  • $250 - Sponsors a month of discipleship training
  • $500 - Supports outreach events to share the Gospel

New Life Foundation is committed to financial integrity and transparency. 85% of all donations go directly to program services, with only 15% used for administrative and fundraising expenses.

We are audited annually and provide detailed financial reports to all donors upon request.
